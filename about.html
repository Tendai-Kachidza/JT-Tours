Who We Are?

we're more than just a tour company; we're a team of passionate adventurers and local experts dedicated to revealing the magic of the Falkland Islands. Our love for these unique islands runs deep, fueled by years of exploring their wild beauty, rich history, and incredible wildlife. We believe that truly experiencing the Falklands means connecting with its heart and soul, and that's exactly what we strive to offer.
Our guides aren't just knowledgeable; they're storytellers, naturalists, and enthusiastic companions committed to making your journey unforgettable. We're here to ensure your Falklands adventure is not only seamless and informative but also filled with genuine moments of wonder and discovery.

Why Choose Us?

JT Tours - when planning your once-in-a-lifetime journey to the Falkland Islands, choosing JT Tours means opting for an unparalleled experience. We distinguish ourselves through our deep local expertise, with guides who aren't just knowledgeable but genuinely passionate about sharing the islands' hidden gems and captivating stories. Unlike larger operations, we specialize in intimate, small-group tours, ensuring personalized attention and more meaningful encounters with the incredible wildlife and dramatic landscapes. Our commitment to responsible tourism means your adventure will be both unforgettable and conducted with the utmost respect for the islands' fragile ecosystem. With JT Tours, you're not simply booking a trip; you're securing a meticulously crafted, authentic exploration led by those who truly know and love the Falklands.
